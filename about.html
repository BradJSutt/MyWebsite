About Me

I'm Brad Sutton, I'm an artist and IT professional. I love creating art and exploring the latest in technology. My passion for learning and growing is the bridge between these two seemingly polar opposite fields.


I've spent the past 10 years working in film and animation, art is something I'll always be passionate about. Now however, it's time for a new journey into the field of IT and cybersecurity. Although, it's not really all that new to me. I grew up right at the big boom of the internet and computers as we know them today (Dial-up was a blast). I would race home every evening and weekend, spend every summer break hooked into a big CRT monitor. I couldnt get enough of learning how this magic device worked, and they ways it could connect all my friends.


Now I'm building on this foundation by completing certifications, building projects, and working towards an accelerated CyberSecurity degree.
